## 2. Principle

StringLab is an app for measuring the string tension of badminton rackets. Protecting your data is important to me. This privacy policy explains what data the app processes and why.

---

## 3. Data Collected and Purpose

### 3.1 Microphone

StringLab requires access to your device's microphone to detect the sound produced when plucking a string and to calculate the string tension.

| | |
|---|---|
| **What is recorded?** | Short audio samples during an active measurement session (a few seconds). |
| **What is stored?** | Only the calculated result (frequency in Hz and tension in kg). The audio data itself is **not** stored, not shared, and not analysed beyond the immediate calculation. |
| **Third-party sharing** | None. All audio processing happens entirely locally on your device. |

### 3.2 App Data (SwiftData / Local Storage)

Data you enter — racket names, string types, measurement results — is stored exclusively locally on your device via Apple's SwiftData framework.

- There is **no synchronisation** with external servers.
- Your data does not leave your device unless you use your operating system's iCloud Backup feature.

### 3.3 No Analytics or Tracking

StringLab does **not** use:

- Any analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel)
- Any advertising networks
- Any third-party crash reporting services
- Any persistent identifiers or device fingerprinting

## 5. Third-Party Disclosure

No personal or usage-related data is shared with any third parties.

## 6. Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device and is protected by the security mechanisms of the operating system (iOS/macOS). StringLab has no server and no access to your stored data.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Since StringLab does not store any personal data on external servers, all data is entirely under your control:

- **Deletion:** All app data is deleted when you uninstall the app.
- **Access:** Since no data is processed outside your device, there is no server-side data to provide access to.

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under the GDPR (e.g. right to information, correction, deletion).

---

## 8. Children

StringLab is not directed at children under the age of 13. No personal data from children is knowingly collected.
