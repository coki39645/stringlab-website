**Measure your badminton racket string tension — quickly and accurately.**

StringLab is an iOS and macOS app that lets you measure the string tension of your badminton racket using only your device's microphone. Simply pluck the string, and StringLab calculates the tension in kg or lbs within seconds.

---

## Features

- **Instant measurement** — detect string tension by sound in real time
- **Racket management** — track multiple rackets with their strings and target tensions
- **Measurement history** — review past measurements and spot tension loss over time
- **Compare rackets** — side-by-side view of two rackets or two stringing setups
- **Colour-coded status** — instantly see which racket needs restringing

---

## Privacy

StringLab respects your privacy. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.
